Privacy & Terms Overview

At Saidos Workflow, data protection and transparency are our highest priorities. This application is a professional tool designed to help creators and agencies manage their social media presence efficiently and securely across all major platforms.

1. Professional Use Case

Saidos Workflow provides an automated content posting bridge for professional social media management. We strictly follow the API guidelines of all connected platforms — including the Meta Platform Terms, YouTube API Services Terms of Service, and TikTok Developer Terms — to ensure a safe and transparent environment for our users.

2. Meta Graph API (Facebook & Instagram)

We integrate the Meta Graph API to publish content to Facebook Pages and Instagram Business accounts on your behalf. We request only the minimum necessary permissions: pages_manage_posts, instagram_content_publish, and pages_read_engagement. We do not access your personal profile, friends list, or private messages. Access tokens are encrypted and never shared with third parties.

3. YouTube Data API v3

We integrate the YouTube Data API v3 (provided by Google LLC) for automated video uploads and channel management. By using this feature, you also agree to the YouTube Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy. You can revoke access at any time via Google Security Settings.

4. TikTok, LinkedIn, Pinterest & X (Twitter)

Our service may additionally connect to TikTok, LinkedIn, Pinterest, and X (Twitter) APIs for content posting. For each platform, we request only the minimum permissions required. Access tokens are stored securely and encrypted, and are never sold or shared with third parties.
