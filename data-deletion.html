Your Right to Data Deletion

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) – specifically Art. 17 GDPR (Right to Erasure / "Right to be Forgotten") – you have the right to request the deletion of all personal data we have collected about you through our social media automation services.

This page is the official data deletion request page for Saidos Workflow and fulfills the data deletion callback requirement for the Meta App Review (Facebook & Instagram). After submitting your request, we will delete all associated data within 30 days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon your request, we will permanently delete all data associated with your connected accounts, including:

  • Your platform user ID and username
  • All stored OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens
  • Connected page IDs and channel IDs
  • Content submitted for publishing (text, images, video metadata)
  • Post scheduling records and error logs
  • Any basic engagement metrics associated with your account

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data in two ways:

  1. Option 1 – Email Request (Recommended) Send an email to herbasaido3@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include the email address or platform username associated with your account. We will confirm deletion within 30 days.
  2. Option 2 – Use the Request Form Below Fill in the form below and submit your deletion request directly. You will receive a confirmation email within 3 business days.
  3. Option 3 – Revoke Access Directly on the Platform You can immediately revoke our access to your social media accounts directly in your platform settings (see links below). This stops all future data processing, but does not delete historical data from our systems — for full deletion, please also submit a request via Option 1 or 2.

Revoke Access Directly on Your Platform

To immediately stop Saidos Workflow from accessing your accounts, revoke access here:

  • Meta Facebook → Settings → Apps and Websites
  • Instagram Instagram → Settings → Apps and Websites
  • YouTube Google Account → Security → Third-party apps with account access
  • TikTok TikTok App → Profile → Settings → Privacy → Connected Apps
  • LinkedIn LinkedIn → Settings → Data Privacy → Permitted Services

What Happens After Your Request

  1. Confirmation (within 3 business days) We will send you a confirmation email acknowledging receipt of your deletion request.
  2. Processing (within 30 days) We will permanently delete all personal data associated with your account from our systems, including access tokens, content records, and log files.
  3. Final Confirmation You will receive a final confirmation email once all your data has been permanently deleted.
Important: Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. After deletion, you will need to reconnect your social media accounts if you wish to use our services again.
